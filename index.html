About me

I am a PhD Candidate in Transportation Engineering at Florida State University. I am currently working Connected Vehicle Infrastructure Systems deployment and testing, Data Analytics and Visualization under supervision of Dr. Ren Moses. As a data scientist, I am interested in parametric and nonparametric models, machine learning methods, deep learning frameworks, cloud computing, statistical modeling techniques and design of data collection experiments.

My practical experience extend from Traffic Operations, Safety application in Bayesian data analytics and Machine learning. I have implemented real-time data collection algorthms for Intelligent Transportation Systems, data analytics and visualization of real life connected vehicles vehicle data.
